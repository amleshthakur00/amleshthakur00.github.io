HCT-Net++: Hybrid Deep Learning Framework for Lung Cancer Detection from CT Images

Amlesh Thakur • Manuscript / journal-paper project • Medical Imaging and Deep Learning

This manuscript focuses on lung cancer detection using CT images and deep learning. The research direction emphasizes model comparison, robust experimental design, explainable AI, and clinically relevant reporting.

Research Focus

  • Hybrid deep learning architecture design
  • Performance comparison against related studies
  • Explainable AI for medical image interpretation
  • Leakage-aware and validation-focused evaluation
