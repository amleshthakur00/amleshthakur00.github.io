About

Professional academic profile for research visibility, collaboration, and publication identity.

Amlesh Thakur is an AI and Data Science researcher whose academic work focuses on deep learning, medical image analysis, computer vision, explainable artificial intelligence, and data-centric evaluation. His research includes CT image classification for kidney stone detection, lung cancer detection, Alzheimer’s disease classification, pneumonia detection, and adaptive multi-object tracking.
